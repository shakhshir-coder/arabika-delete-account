What to expect

Before submitting your request, here's what you should know about the deletion process.

Permanent & irreversible

All your data — including profile information, preferences, saved content, and activity history — will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Processing within 30 days

Your request will be processed within 30 days. During this window you can contact support to cancel the request.

Your rights protected

This process complies with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA. A confirmation email will be sent to you.

By proceeding, you confirm that you understand all associated data will be permanently deleted and this action cannot be undone.
